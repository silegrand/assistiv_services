# Assistiv Services

> Assistiv Services is the preventative care and support layer of the Assistiv ecosystem: living well at home, on your terms. It serves older adults identified through community screening who fall below the acute clinical threshold — the Missing Middle — with person-led preventative care, passive home safety monitoring, care coordination, and community connection. Nothing is installed, monitored or shared without the person's explicit, granular, revocable consent.

Operated by Assistiv Systems Limited, registered in England and Wales, company number 17082597. Contact: simon@assistiv.co.

---

## Philosophy

The sensors are not the product; the relationship is. Assistiv Services is built on Positive Interdependence — the recognition that healthy later life is not solitary self-sufficiency but support freely chosen, on the person's own terms. Technology is introduced only in response to what the person says they need, always reversible, always theirs to refuse.

## Triple Tap Logic

Core design, proprietary intellectual property. No single perspective tells the whole story of how someone is doing. Assistiv brings three together each month — the person's own account, a nominated carer's observation, and clinical findings — across six quality-of-life domains, and pays closest attention to where they differ. Divergence between perspectives is itself a clinical signal.

## Who it serves

- Older adults living independently but at growing risk — the 3.5 million strong Missing Middle
- Families and carers seeking reassurance without surveillance
- NHS commissioners, ICBs, PCNs, local authorities and housing providers working in early intervention and ageing-in-place

## Position in the ecosystem

Assistiv Services is Layer 6 (Serve) of the seven-layer Assistiv pathway: people identified through assistiv.cloud population intelligence and assistiv.tools community screening are supported here, with outcomes feeding back to recalibrate the system.
